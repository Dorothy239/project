Privacy Policy for Book Haven

Effective Date: 30th March 2025

Introduction

At Book Haven, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to ensure your privacy is respected.

By using Book Haven, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Book Haven, we collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account on Book Haven, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, profile picture, and any other details you provide in your profile.
  • User Content: We collect information that you voluntarily provide, including book reviews, ratings, lists, comments, and messages.
  • Payment Information: If you make any purchases or donations on the platform (e.g., books or premium features), we may collect payment details such as credit card information. However, we use a third-party payment processor for handling financial transactions, and we do not store sensitive payment data directly.

Non-Personal Information

  • Usage Data: We automatically collect data about how you interact with the platform. This may include information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These tools help us remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, and provide personalized content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our services, such as allowing you to create and manage your account, post reviews, and interact with other users.
  • To personalize your experience, such as offering book recommendations and content based on your reading preferences.
  • To communicate with you, including sending notifications about your account, updates on new book releases, or changes to our services.
  • To improve the functionality and performance of the website by analyzing usage data and making enhancements.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate and maintain the platform, such as payment processors, hosting services, or customer support tools. These providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purpose of providing their services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, such as a court or government agency.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction, but we will notify you before any such transfer takes place.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the services you requested, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you decide to delete your account, your personal information will be removed from our systems within a reasonable time, except as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain rights regarding the personal data we hold about you:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal obligations or legitimate business reasons we may have for retaining it.
  • Objection to Processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes, such as marketing or profiling.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email address].

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on Book Haven. A cookie is a small file that is stored on your device to help remember your preferences, login details, and browsing activities. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our site.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page, and the new version will be effective as of the date indicated at the top of the policy.
